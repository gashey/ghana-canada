Who We Are

The Canada Ghana Chamber of Commerce – Calgary Chapter (CGCC–Calgary) is a results-oriented, globally engaged business chamber focused on strengthening bilateral economic relations between Canada and Ghana. As part of the larger Canada Ghana Chamber of Commerce (CANCHAM) network, we serve as a pivotal platform connecting Alberta’s global industrial and innovation hub with West Africa’s emerging markets.
