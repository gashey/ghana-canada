Our Partners

We act as a strategic connector of entrepreneurs, executives, institutions, and policymakers, creating cross-border trade opportunities, expanding professional networks, and facilitating impactful investment partnerships. CGCC-Calgary brings over 30 years of collective expertise in fostering public-private partnerships, regional integration, and diaspora-led development initiatives.

Our operations are powered by strategic relationships with institutions across Canada, Ghana, and beyond
