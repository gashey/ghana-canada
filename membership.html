Why Join CGCC Calgary?

We are not only a Chamber, we are a strategic launchpad for global business success. Our leadership team offers cross-sectoral expertise, deep diplomatic relationships, and hands-on experience in deal facilitation, strategic policy engagement, and community development. Whether you're a company looking to expand to Ghana, a policymaker seeking partnerships, or a young entrepreneur building your global footprint, CGCC-Calgary is your trusted partner.

Who Can Join?

We welcome individuals and organizations from across the spectrum of public and private sectors including:

• Entrepreneurs, SMEs, and corporate entities

• Professionals, consultants, and trade advisors

• Government agencies and public institutions

• Investment firms, financiers, and export houses

• Educational, research, and development institutions

• Diaspora networks and youth-led enterprises

Become A Member

Join the Canada Ghana Chamber of Commerce - Calgary Chapter and gain exclusive access to a powerful business network, strategic partnerships, bilateral trade missions, and market insights across Canada and Ghana.

1

Step 1: Choose Your Membership Package

Review the membership plans above and select the package that best aligns with your business goals and needs. Each tier offers different levels of access and benefits.

2

Step 2: Register Online

To streamline your membership process, click the appropriate link below to fill out your secure application form.

• Individual / SME / Corporate Registration Form

• Strategic Partner / Institutional Membership Form

• VIP & VVIP Membership Application

3

Step 3 : Payment & Activation

Once you submit your application, a confirmation email with payment details and onboarding instructions will be sent to your registered email.

• Bank transfer or mobile money options available

• Email confirmation within 24-48 hours

4

Step 4 : Member Onboarding

After payment confirmation.

• Receive your digital membership certificate

• Access member directory login

• Begin receiving newsletters and exclusive invitations
